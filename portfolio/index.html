Ricky Podgorski

About

My name is Ricky Podgorski. I am a dedicated and driven leader with a passion for sports broadcasting. I am a current student at the Philip Merrill College of Journalism at the University of Maryland. In my time at Merrill, I have developed numerous skills ranging from video shooting, to on-air anchoring, and live reporting. I am currently an Executive Producer of Terrapin Sports Central, a sports media outlet, covering all forms of Maryland athletics. As an Executive Producer, I lead our organization in the coverage of games, on-air talent, producing segments and packages for studio shows, and creating social media content. Additionally, I have worked as a crew member for the University of Maryland Big Ten Student U helping in the production of Maryland athletics for the conference as a camera operator. I also have experience with ESPN as a crew member assisting camera operators, on-air talent, and audio technicians with the production of XFL broadcasts. Outside of the University of Maryland, I have been involved in many other roles. In the summer of 2023, I acted as the Sideline Reporter for the Danbury Westerners Baseball Club of the New England Collegiate Baseball League. With the Westerners, I was selected as the 2023 NECBL All-Star Sideline Reporter where I was given the opportunity to cover the 2023 NECBL All-Star game which was broadcasted to over 75 million households across the United States. Prior to college, I created leadership opportunities and experiences in high school in Farmington Connecticut. For three years I represented my graduating class as the Class President. I also acted as the Editor-in-Chief of the school newspaper the FHS Voice, and was the lead anchor of the school news broadcast, the 9:05 news. I served as the team captain for the state championship-winning Farmington High School Basketball team.
